# Copywriting

> Principles for persuasive and conversion-optimized writing.

---

## 1. Core Principles

| Principle | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| **Know Your Audience** | Write specifically for their desires, problems, and language. |
| **Benefits > Features** | Ask "So what?". Highlight how the product improves their life. |
| **Clarity & Conciseness** | Clear, direct language. Avoid jargon and fluff. |
| **Strong CTA** | One clear goal per piece of copy. Tell them exactly what to do next. |
| **Emotional Connection** | People buy on emotion and justify with logic. Use storytelling. |
| **Credibility & Trust** | Use social proof, facts, and authentic claims. |

---

## 2. Proven Frameworks

### P.A.S. (Problem, Agitate, Solution)
Best for: Landing pages, emails, social ads.
- **Problem**: Identify the specific pain point.
- **Agitate**: Emphasize how bad the problem is or what happens if it's ignored.
- **Solution**: Present your product/service as the perfect fix.

### A.I.D.A. (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action)
Best for: Long-form sales pages, video scripts, funnels.
- **Attention**: Hook the reader immediately with a strong headline.
- **Interest**: Keep them reading with fascinating facts or stories.
- **Desire**: Make them want the solution (benefits).
- **Action**: Tell them exactly how to get it.

### B.A.B. (Before-After-Bridge)
Best for: Cold outreach, social media, blogging.
- **Before**: Their current world with the problem.
- **After**: The ideal world once the problem is solved.
- **Bridge**: How your product gets them there.

### F.A.B. (Features, Advantages, Benefits)
Best for: Product descriptions.
- **Features**: What it is or does.
- **Advantages**: Why it's better or helpful.
- **Benefits**: The ultimate emotional or practical payoff for the user.

## 3. The Power of Headlines

> The headline is 80% of the battle. If they don't read the headline, they won't read the copy.

| Type | Example |
|------|---------|
| **How-To** | How to Write Copy That Converts Like Crazy |
| **Listicle** | 7 Copywriting Secrets Every Marketer Should Know |
| **Question** | Are You Making These Costly Landing Page Mistakes? |
| **Secret/Mystery**| The One Word That Doubles Your Conversion Rate |
| **Testimonial** | "This Simple Framework Made Me $10k in A Week" |

---

## 4. Copywriting Checklist

Before publishing, ensure the copy answers:
- [ ] Is it clear and conversational? (Read it out loud)
- [ ] Is it focused on ONE specific reader?
- [ ] Does it focus on benefits rather than just features?
- [ ] Is the active voice used predominantly?
- [ ] Is there a clear, single Call-To-Action (CTA)?
- [ ] Have all unnecessary words been edited out?

---

> **Remember:** Good copy is a conversation, not a lecture. It solves a problem and guides the reader to a solution.
